Guide
01

Click Roll Dice button

To get started, click the roll dice button on the home page. This button will direct you to select roller Level.
02

Select roller level

Each roller level has a price tag on it. Make sure you select what you can afford. After selecting your roller level, you will be directed to a payment page.
03

Successful Payment

If your payment is successful, you will be directed to a dice page. If your payment is not successful, you will be directed back to the home page.
04

Roll Dice

Click the Roll Dice button to roll dice. You can roll the dice 2 times only, after which the button will be disabled. The result of your rolls will be displayed on the screen in the 'Money back' dialog box.
05

Roll Dice Rules!!! IMPORTANT!!!

i. You have only 3 minutes to roll dice and cash out. If you do not roll and cashout within the stipulated time, you will be logged out and you will lose your money
ii. You must not refresh the dice page.If you refresh the dice page you will be logged out and you will lose your money
06

Cash Out

After rolling the dice twice, click the cashout button. Provide your information and cashout. Make sure the information provided in the cashout form matches the information provided on the payment page.
06

Return to home

After clicking the cash out button, click the return to home at the top left of the page. Sit back and expect your money back
07

Need Help?

If you need any help contact support@rolldice.xyz
